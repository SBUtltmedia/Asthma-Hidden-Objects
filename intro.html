Welcome to the Asthma Trigger House!
   The Asthma Trigger House is an interactive learning tool for increasing your ability to identify triggers that may cause asthma symptoms as well as ways to avoid them. You will enter through the hallway where you will learn how to use this tool. The hallway is the place where you will be given instructions. Here you will practice going through the four rooms of this special house in a set order. HAVE FUN!!
